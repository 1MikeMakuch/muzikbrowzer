Remote Control operation

Depending upon your application there are numerous remote control solutions from which to choose.

For direct control of Muzikbrowzer with a remote control you must purchase and install one of the supported IR devices, discussed below.

A more general solution are the keyboard/mouse emulator programs/devices. These general purpose products generate virtual keystrokes and allow you to control your pc with a remote control. Muzikbrowzer supports these products by virtue of the keyboard shortcuts defined for all functions.

Another option is a wireless keyboard/mouse.

Many pc network remote control solutions exist. There are many "pc remote" control solutions that allow you to remotely control a pc from another pc. Products like PC Anywhere.

Supported IR devices

These IR devices are directly supported by Muzikbrowzer.

  • Irman
  • USB-UIRT
  • Tira (coming soon)

Each of these devices retails for under $50 and are "universal" infra red receiving devices. Meaning that they generally will work with any IR remote control. They do not come with a remote control. The idea is you use one of your own, perhaps an old one not being used.

Irman. Requires a serial port (not usb). Just plug it into your pc and proceed with training Muzikbrowzer. Do not install any software drivers that may come with Irman.

USB-UIRT plugs into a usb port. You must 1st install the USB-UIRT Windows drivers before Muzikbrowzer can communicate with it. Follow their instructions for installing their USB-UIRT and Windows driver.

Tira plugs into a usb port. You must 1st install the Tira Windows drivers before Muzikbrowzer can communicate with it. Follow their instructions for installing their Tira and Windows driver.

After you've installed your IR device and any necessary drivers proceed to training Muzikbrowzer with your remote control in Settings/Remote Control.
