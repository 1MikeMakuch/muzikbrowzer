Quick Start

  • Purchase and Install muzikbrowzer!
  • Plug your Irman infrared receiver into an available COM port on the back of your pc.
  • Run muzikbrowzer and click on Configuration from the Menu button
  • On the Files tab select the directories where your mp3 music files are stored on your computer or network. muzikbrowzer will recursively scan these directories for .mp3 files.
  • Click the scan button. muzikbrowzer will scan the directories you selected for mp3 files and build it's own database.
  • On the Irman tab select the COM port for the Irman receiver.
  • Teach muzikbrowzer your remote control codes. Click the Record button then click on the "Up" button. Now press the button on your remote control that will serve as the "Up" button.
  • Repeat for all remaining buttons
  • Click OK to save
  • Begin enjoying your music!
Trial Mode
  • When you first install muzikbrowzer it defaults to Trial Mode.
  • You have 60 days to try out muzikbrowzer before deciding whether or not to purchase
  • Trial Mode gives you full functionality, except that it will play only three songs and then stop. You may then press Play to continue playing more songs in the Playlist. You'll just have to press Play after every third song in Trial Mode.
  • After 60 days muzikbrowzer will cease to operate, unless you purchase it.
  • At any time during or after the Trial period you may go to www.muzikbrowzer.com and select the Purchase link to make the purchase. At that point you'll be given a license password which will unlock Trial Mode permanently.
Operation
  • Remote Control
    • Up/Down/Left/Right navigate between Genres,Artists,Songs,Playlist
    • Select selects the highlighted Genre,Artist,Album or Song and adds it to the Playlist. The first song begins playing
    • Menu brings up a menu of choices available with the remote control
  • Keyboard/Mouse
    • Tab, arrows and mouse operate in usual Windows fashion.
    • Click on Menu to gain access to additional functions not available with the remote control, i.e. Configuration settings, Save Playlist.
