Quick Start

  • Download and Install Muzikbrowzer.
  • Run Muzikbrowzer and click on Settings from the Menu button
  • On the Files tab select the folders where your audio music files are stored on your computer or network. Muzikbrowzer will recursively scan these folders for audio files.
  • Click Ok. Go to Menu/MusicLibrary/Rebuild/Complete. Muzikbrowzer will search the folders you selected for audio files and build it's own database.
  • Begin enjoying your music!

Requires Windows Media Player 9 or later.

Optional Muzikbrowzer may be controlled via remote control. This capability requires purchase of a supported remote control infrared receiver device: Irman, USB-UIRT, Tira. See the Remote Control chapter for further information. Muzikbrowzer may also work with keyboard emulator types of remote control solutions.

Trial Mode
  • When you first install Muzikbrowzer it defaults to Trial Mode.
  • You have 60 days to try out Muzikbrowzer before deciding whether or not to purchase
  • Trial Mode gives you full functionality, except that it will play only three songs and then stop. You may then press Play to continue playing more songs in the Playlist. You'll just have to press Play after every third song in Trial Mode.
  • After 60 days Muzikbrowzer will cease to operate, unless you purchase it.
  • At any time during or after the Trial period you may go to www.muzikbrowzer.com and select the Purchase link to make the purchase. At that point you'll be given a license key which will unlock Trial Mode permanently.
Operation
  • Remote Control
    • Up/Down/Left/Right navigate between Genres,Artists,Songs,Playlist
    • Select selects the highlighted Genre,Artist,Album or Song and adds it to the Playlist. The first song begins playing
    • Menu brings up a menu of choices available with the remote control
  • Keyboard/Mouse
    • Tab, arrows and mouse operate in usual Windows fashion.
    • Click on Menu to gain access to additional functions not available with the remote control, i.e. Configuration settings, Save Playlist.
