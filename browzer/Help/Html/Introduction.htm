Welcome

Welcome to muzikbrowzer. This brief help document will tell you everything you need to know to begin playing music from your music collection. Muzikbrowzer plays mp3 and wma audio files. It will also play .ogg files, an open source audio format. .ogg files require an additional open source component - see Help Configuration.

muzikbrowzer is designed to revolutionize the way you play music from your home entertainment center, from the comfort of your easy chair with a remote control. With muzikbrowzer you can now quickly browse through your entire music library and begin playing music with just a few button presses on your remote control.

What's new in version 2.0?

  • Skins
  • More user definable display settings
  • New improved screen layout, adjustable Library column widths
  • Search
  • Now plays wma's - Windows Media Player format audio files.
